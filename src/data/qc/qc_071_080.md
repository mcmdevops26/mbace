4. **The private banker revenue per banker figure ($3-5M)** in Step 5 appears low. If a private banker manages 30-50 UHNW client families at $25M+ AUM minimum, at 1.4% fee yield, one family generates $350K annually. Thirty families = $10.5M in annual revenue per banker. Even netting out operating costs, the $3-5M figure likely refers to contribution margin not revenue. The distinction should be made explicit.
5. **MECE is clean.** Strategic Positioning, Financial Impact by Path, and Competitive/Execution Risk are mutually exclusive and cover the decision well.
6. **The "double down on UHNW, digitize mass-affluent" recommendation is well-reasoned** and the SPECIAL-T application to wealth management (robo-advisor commoditization, FATCA as barrier to entry) is genuinely insightful and specific.
